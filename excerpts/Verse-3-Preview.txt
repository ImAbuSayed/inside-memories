Hanere de vich lukeya haan main banke dark shadow,
Tainu labh ke main khid janda haan wangu green meadow,
Meri akhiyan de vich hanju aunde ne tears fall,
Main kalla baitha ronda haan vekh ke ae blank wall.
Loki kehnde mainu aashiq haan main taan crazy guy,
Main pyar de vich dubb gaya haan utte deep sky,
Tu mud ke na vekhe mainu rakhe tu cold heart,
Main karda haan yaad tainu reh ke vi miles apart.
