Kanch de glass vich mildi ae mainu cold vibe,
Main kalla baitha labhda haan tera sohna face,
Kaale asmaan de vich chamke tu kalli bright star,
Dil dharkada ae mera sunda haan main slow pace.
Teri mithi jehi khushbu ae kudiye my breath,
Main karda haan udeek teri kalli kalli no rest,
Pyaar wali aag vich jalda haan main deep down,
Tere karke main chad ditta sara hi whole town.
