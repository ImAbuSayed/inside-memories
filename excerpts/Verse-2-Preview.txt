Vadde engine di awaaz ghuje sadkaan te loud roar,
Main sadkaan te kalla bhajda haan labhda new door,
Teri zehari jehi akhaan mainu kardi ae do kill,
Main jhalla jeha firda haan tere piche with thrill.
Pakke vaade karke main nibhanda haan ban real man,
Tu jhuthi jehi kasmaan khaawe banake ik fake plan,
Lohe di mashin wangu bhajda haan main top gear,
Tere nakhre nu has has sehnda haan without fear.
